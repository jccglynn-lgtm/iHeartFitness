About Diet

Eating is a part of a healthy lifestyle in our life. It provides nutrition and micronutrients, which help our body to be strong and fit. They also impact our mood and energy level. Wrong food choices can lead to health problems like obesity, diabetes, and heart diseases. So, the right diet is very important for our life.

Here are some tips for a healthy diet:

  • Eat a variety of foods: Include fruits, vegetables, whole grains, lean proteins, and healthy fats in your diet.
  • Control portion sizes: Be mindful of portion sizes to avoid overeating.
  • Limit processed foods: Reduce intake of processed and sugary foods.
  • Stay hydrated: Drink plenty of water throughout the day.
  • Plan meals: Prepare meals in advance to ensure balanced nutrition.

About BMI

BMI (Body Mass Index) is a simple measure of body fat based on height and weight, used for adults to check if their weight is healthy.

BMI Categories:

  • Underweight: BMI less than 18.5
  • Normal weight: BMI 18.5 - 24.9
  • Overweight: BMI 25 - 29.9
  • Obese: BMI 30 or greater
